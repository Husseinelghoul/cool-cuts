25 Years Experience

Welcome to our barber shop - a place where you'll find the best haircuts in town! With over 25 years of experience, our professional barbers know how to create the perfect look for you. We're located in the heart of Hamra, and have been a part of this community for decades.

Our prices are unbeatable, and we're proud to have earned a reputation as the go-to destination for stylish haircuts. We cater to everyone, from busy professionals to students, and we'll always go the extra mile to make sure you're satisfied with your look.

Our shop has even been visited by important people, including celebrities, politicians, and business owners. But we treat every customer like a VIP, and you'll leave feeling like a new person. Our barbers are friendly and personable, and we guarantee you'll have fun while getting the best haircut of your life.

In addition to our in-store services, we also offer in-home hair trimming appointments. So if you're too busy to come to us, we'll come to you! Contact us to schedule an appointment, or stop by our shop today to experience our exceptional service firsthand.
